About Us (TGI)

Tontine Group International, located in Ajman, U.A.E., specializes in the import and export of precious metals, specifically Gold and Diamonds. Our primary objective through this business plan is to secure a funding of US$ 1 Billion, with the aim of engaging in the trading of valuable metals and minerals. This plan also outlines our projected financial and operational performance for the upcoming five years. The market for precious metals remains highly profitable on a global scale, and our strategic goal is to position ourselves to capitalize on the numerous opportunities within this sector. Our business strategy takes into account an analysis of the present and future economic landscape, as well as the competitive environment within the broader Gold Trading Business context. In this plan, Tontine Group International sets forth its vision for the next half-decade and formulates a mission statement that will steer our operations with an unwavering commitment to excellence.
